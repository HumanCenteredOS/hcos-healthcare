# 🏥 HCOS™ Healthcare

## Human-Centered Operating Systems™

Healthcare exists to reduce suffering.

HCOS™ Healthcare applies the principles of Human-Centered Operating Systems™ to improve healthcare by strengthening the relationships between people, workflows, technology, and organizations.

Our goal is to help healthcare professionals, patients, caregivers, administrators, researchers, educators, and technology partners create healthier systems that improve both patient outcomes and the human experience of care.

---

# ❤️ Our Purpose

Healthcare is one of the most complex human systems.

Every day, clinicians, patients, caregivers, and organizations navigate competing priorities, limited resources, changing technology, and increasing complexity.

HCOS™ Healthcare provides a human-centered framework that helps healthcare teams:

- Improve decision-making
- Reduce unnecessary human burden
- Strengthen communication and collaboration
- Design healthier workflows
- Implement AI responsibly
- Improve quality, safety, and patient experience
- Support clinician well-being
- Create conditions where people and organizations can flourish
